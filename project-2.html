Credit Card Monthly Payment Calculator

Credit cards are confusing and many people aimlessly may payments. This tool helps people build a plan on how to pay off their credit card and visualizes it

Project Overview

This is a financial literacy tool which is meant to help pay off a credit card and visualize metrics regarding that pay off process

Home page

The main page of the tool has 4 form inputs: Total Balance, APR, Max Months/Max Monthly Payment, and Extra Monthly Spend. There are two options for the third input because some people have a max monthly limit they can contribute towards a payment. In this case we calculate the total months it will take. On the other hand, some people want to specify a number of months they want it paid off as and in this case we generate the monthly payment.

Once you hit submit, it will either generate the total months, or the monthly payment. It will also show you the total amount of money paid in interest until the card is paid off. Under that you can see 3 graphs. The first shows the breaktown of interest vs principal. The second shows each monthly payment and the breakdown of how much goes to interest vs principa. The last one shows the decrease of the principal balance over time.

Compare Feature

In the home page these is a compare button. Once this is hit, the entries in the original form are "locked" and you are allowed to enter 4 more values to compare to. It will show 2 sets of graphs and allow you to compare side to side.
